About

It is a police drama with religious overtones that puts the viewer’s ethics into question by making them witness the darkest side of the mafia behind false churches. We will achieve this through the character of Martin, an anti-hero who decides to confront the system; a man moved by pain and the need to get his life back. Tragically, in his attempt to do so, he drifts further and further away from who he once was.

Roadmap

By doing so, I can tell my story and that of each viewer: each person who has complied with what our society has asked of them and this society has turned its back on them by taking their money, their security, their dreams, their time, and their dignity. I do not intend to narrate about who we think we are or who we fantasize about becoming, but I will expose, through our protagonist, our most vulnerable side, that one which has been hurt and hidden from others. I will expose the wounds so that later, as the audience empathizes with the character, they make a search for redemption and revenge of their own, as well as the doubts and the pain that this implies.
